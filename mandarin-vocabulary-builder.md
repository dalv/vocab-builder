# 1. COMMON VERBS (75 words)

## Wanting, Feeling & Thinking

**想** xiǎng — want to / think / miss
想吃火锅。 Xiǎng chī huǒguō. — I want to eat hotpot.

**要** yào — want / need / going to
我要这个。 Wǒ yào zhège. — I want this one.

**喜欢** xǐhuan — like
我喜欢喝茶。 Wǒ xǐhuan hē chá. — I like drinking tea.

**爱** ài — love
我爱吃辣的。 Wǒ ài chī là de. — I love eating spicy food.

**需要** xūyào — need
我需要你的帮助。 Wǒ xūyào nǐ de bāngzhù. — I need your help.

**知道** zhīdào — know (a fact)
我知道了。 Wǒ zhīdào le. — I know. / Got it.

**认识** rènshi — know (a person) / recognize
你认识他吗？ Nǐ rènshi tā ma? — Do you know him?

**觉得** juéde — think / feel (opinion)
我觉得不错。 Wǒ juéde búcuò. — I think it's pretty good.

**以为** yǐwéi — thought (mistakenly assumed)
我以为你不来了。 Wǒ yǐwéi nǐ bù lái le. — I thought you weren't coming.

**记得** jìde — remember
你记得密码吗？ Nǐ jìde mìmǎ ma? — Do you remember the password?

**忘了** wàng le — forgot
我忘了带钥匙。 Wǒ wàng le dài yàoshi. — I forgot to bring my keys.

**懂** dǒng — understand (comprehend)
听懂了吗？ Tīng dǒng le ma? — Did you understand (what you heard)?

**明白** míngbai — understand (the meaning / reasoning)
我明白你的意思。 Wǒ míngbai nǐ de yìsi. — I understand what you mean.

**相信** xiāngxìn — believe / trust
你相信我。 Nǐ xiāngxìn wǒ. — Trust me.

**希望** xīwàng — hope
希望明天不下雨。 Xīwàng míngtiān bú xiàyǔ. — I hope it doesn't rain tomorrow.

**怕** pà — afraid / scared of
我怕蛇。 Wǒ pà shé. — I'm scared of snakes.

**担心** dānxīn — worry
别担心。 Bié dānxīn. — Don't worry.

**高兴** gāoxìng — happy / glad
认识你很高兴。 Rènshi nǐ hěn gāoxìng. — Glad to meet you.

**生气** shēngqì — angry
他生气了。 Tā shēngqì le. — He got angry.

**后悔** hòuhuǐ — regret
我有点后悔。 Wǒ yǒudiǎn hòuhuǐ. — I kind of regret it.


## Doing & Moving

**去** qù — go
你去哪儿？ Nǐ qù nǎr? — Where are you going?

**来** lái — come
你什么时候来？ Nǐ shénme shíhou lái? — When are you coming?

**回** huí — return / go back
我要回家了。 Wǒ yào huí jiā le. — I'm going to head home.

**走** zǒu — walk / leave / go
我们走吧。 Wǒmen zǒu ba. — Let's go.

**跑** pǎo — run
他每天早上跑步。 Tā měitiān zǎoshang pǎobù. — He runs every morning.

**到** dào — arrive / reach
到了吗？ Dào le ma? — Have you arrived?

**进** jìn — enter / go in
请进。 Qǐng jìn. — Please come in.

**出** chū — go out / exit
我出去一下。 Wǒ chūqù yíxià. — I'm going out for a bit.

**住** zhù — live / stay
你住在哪里？ Nǐ zhù zài nǎlǐ? — Where do you live?

**搬** bān — move (to a new place)
我刚搬到这里。 Wǒ gāng bān dào zhèlǐ. — I just moved here.

**带** dài — bring / carry / take along
记得带伞。 Jìde dài sǎn. — Remember to bring an umbrella.

**拿** ná — hold / grab / take
帮我拿一下。 Bāng wǒ ná yíxià. — Hold this for me a sec.

**放** fàng — put / place
放在桌子上吧。 Fàng zài zhuōzi shàng ba. — Put it on the table.

**送** sòng — send / deliver / give (as gift) / see someone off
我送你到地铁站。 Wǒ sòng nǐ dào dìtiě zhàn. — I'll walk you to the metro.

**寄** jì — mail / send (a package)
我要寄一个包裹。 Wǒ yào jì yí ge bāoguǒ. — I want to mail a package.

**找** zhǎo — look for / search
我在找我的手机。 Wǒ zài zhǎo wǒ de shǒujī. — I'm looking for my phone.

**见面** jiànmiàn — meet (someone)
我们明天见面吧。 Wǒmen míngtiān jiànmiàn ba. — Let's meet tomorrow.

**等** děng — wait
等一下。 Děng yíxià. — Wait a moment.

**站** zhàn — stand
站在这里别动。 Zhàn zài zhèlǐ bié dòng. — Stand here, don't move.

**坐** zuò — sit
随便坐。 Suíbiàn zuò. — Sit anywhere you like.


## Daily Life

**吃** chī — eat
你吃了吗？ Nǐ chī le ma? — Have you eaten?

**喝** hē — drink
喝点什么？ Hē diǎn shénme? — What would you like to drink?

**睡觉** shuìjiào — sleep
昨天睡得太晚了。 Zuótiān shuì de tài wǎn le. — I slept too late last night.

**起床** qǐchuáng — get up / wake up
我每天六点起床。 Wǒ měitiān liù diǎn qǐchuáng. — I get up at six every day.

**洗澡** xǐzǎo — shower / bathe
我先洗个澡。 Wǒ xiān xǐ ge zǎo. — Let me take a shower first.

**做饭** zuòfàn — cook
今天谁做饭？ Jīntiān shéi zuòfàn? — Who's cooking today?

**点菜** diǎncài — order food
我们可以点菜了。 Wǒmen kěyǐ diǎncài le. — We can order now.

**付钱** fùqián — pay
在哪儿付钱？ Zài nǎr fùqián? — Where do I pay?

**买** mǎi — buy
我在网上买的。 Wǒ zài wǎngshàng mǎi de. — I bought it online.

**卖** mài — sell
这个还卖吗？ Zhège hái mài ma? — Is this still for sale?

**租** zū — rent
我租了一个房子。 Wǒ zū le yí ge fángzi. — I rented a place.

**工作** gōngzuò — work
你在哪儿工作？ Nǐ zài nǎr gōngzuò? — Where do you work?

**休息** xiūxi — rest / take a break
我们休息一会儿吧。 Wǒmen xiūxi yíhuìr ba. — Let's take a break.

**锻炼** duànliàn — exercise / work out
你平时怎么锻炼？ Nǐ píngshí zěnme duànliàn? — How do you usually exercise?


## Communication

**说** shuō — say / speak
你说什么？ Nǐ shuō shénme? — What did you say?

**告诉** gàosu — tell
告诉我怎么走。 Gàosu wǒ zěnme zǒu. — Tell me how to get there.

**聊** liáo — chat
我们聊一聊吧。 Wǒmen liáo yi liáo ba. — Let's have a chat.

**问** wèn — ask
我问你一个问题。 Wǒ wèn nǐ yí ge wèntí. — Let me ask you a question.

**回答** huídá — answer / reply
他还没回答我。 Tā hái méi huídá wǒ. — He still hasn't answered me.

**听** tīng — listen / hear
你听我说。 Nǐ tīng wǒ shuō. — Listen to me.

**看** kàn — look / see / watch / read
看一下这个。 Kàn yíxià zhège. — Take a look at this.

**叫** jiào — call (someone) / be called
我叫 Vlad。 Wǒ jiào Vlad. — My name is Vlad.

**打电话** dǎ diànhuà — make a phone call
我晚点给你打电话。 Wǒ wǎn diǎn gěi nǐ dǎ diànhuà. — I'll call you later.

**发** fā — send (a message / text)
我发微信给你。 Wǒ fā wēixìn gěi nǐ. — I'll send you a WeChat message.


## Actions & States

**能** néng — can (physical ability / circumstances)
今天不能来。 Jīntiān bù néng lái. — I can't come today.

**可以** kěyǐ — can / may (permission)
这里可以拍照吗？ Zhèlǐ kěyǐ pāizhào ma? — Can I take photos here?

**会** huì — can (learned skill) / will (probability)
你会做饭吗？ Nǐ huì zuòfàn ma? — Can you cook?

**应该** yīnggāi — should
你应该试试。 Nǐ yīnggāi shìshi. — You should try it.

**试** shì — try
你试一下。 Nǐ shì yíxià. — Give it a try.

**学** xué — learn / study
我在学中文。 Wǒ zài xué zhōngwén. — I'm studying Chinese.

**教** jiāo — teach
谁教你中文？ Shéi jiāo nǐ zhōngwén? — Who teaches you Chinese?

**帮** bāng — help
你能帮我吗？ Nǐ néng bāng wǒ ma? — Can you help me?

**用** yòng — use
这个怎么用？ Zhège zěnme yòng? — How do you use this?

**做** zuò — do / make
你在做什么？ Nǐ zài zuò shénme? — What are you doing?

**开** kāi — open / turn on / drive
门开着呢。 Mén kāi zhe ne. — The door is open.

**关** guān — close / turn off
帮我关一下灯。 Bāng wǒ guān yíxià dēng. — Help me turn off the light.

**开始** kāishǐ — start / begin
几点开始？ Jǐ diǎn kāishǐ? — What time does it start?

**完** wán — finish / done
吃完了。 Chī wán le. — Finished eating.

**换** huàn — change / swap / exchange
我想换个座位。 Wǒ xiǎng huàn ge zuòwèi. — I'd like to change my seat.

**选** xuǎn — choose / pick
你选哪个？ Nǐ xuǎn nǎge? — Which one do you pick?

**有** yǒu — have / there is
有WiFi吗？ Yǒu WiFi ma? — Is there WiFi?

**没有** méiyǒu — don't have / there isn't
没有了。 Méiyǒu le. — There's none left.

**坏了** huài le — broken
空调坏了。 Kōngtiáo huài le. — The AC is broken.

**修** xiū — fix / repair
能修吗？ Néng xiū ma? — Can it be fixed?

**给** gěi — give / for (someone)
给你。 Gěi nǐ. — Here you go. / This is for you.

**借** jiè — borrow / lend
能借你的充电器吗？ Néng jiè nǐ de chōngdiànqì ma? — Can I borrow your charger?

# 2. ADJECTIVE PAIRS (35 pairs)

*Chinese adjectives typically need 很 (hěn) before them in simple statements: "他很高" (He is tall), not just "他高".*

## Size & Quantity

**大** dà ↔ **小** xiǎo — big ↔ small
这个房间很大。 Zhège fángjiān hěn dà. — This room is big.
要小份的。 Yào xiǎo fèn de. — I want the small portion.

**长** cháng ↔ **短** duǎn — long ↔ short
路很长。 Lù hěn cháng. — The road is long.
时间太短了。 Shíjiān tài duǎn le. — The time is too short.

**高** gāo ↔ **矮** ǎi / **低** dī — tall/high ↔ short/low
那栋楼很高。 Nà dòng lóu hěn gāo. — That building is really tall.
价格比较低。 Jiàgé bǐjiào dī. — The price is relatively low.

**宽** kuān ↔ **窄** zhǎi — wide ↔ narrow
马路很宽。 Mǎlù hěn kuān. — The road is wide.
这条巷子很窄。 Zhè tiáo xiàngzi hěn zhǎi. — This alley is very narrow.

**厚** hòu ↔ **薄** báo — thick ↔ thin
这件衣服太厚了。 Zhè jiàn yīfu tài hòu le. — This clothing is too thick.
饼太薄了。 Bǐng tài báo le. — The pancake is too thin.

**多** duō ↔ **少** shǎo — many/much ↔ few/little
人太多了。 Rén tài duō le. — Too many people.
少放点盐。 Shǎo fàng diǎn yán. — Put less salt.

**满** mǎn ↔ **空** kōng — full ↔ empty
地铁满了。 Dìtiě mǎn le. — The metro is full.
旁边有个空位。 Pángbiān yǒu ge kōng wèi. — There's an empty seat next to it.

**重** zhòng ↔ **轻** qīng — heavy ↔ light
箱子太重了。 Xiāngzi tài zhòng le. — The suitcase is too heavy.
这个很轻。 Zhège hěn qīng. — This one is light.


## Quality & State

**好** hǎo ↔ **坏** huài — good ↔ bad
天气真好。 Tiānqì zhēn hǎo. — The weather is really nice.
心情很坏。 Xīnqíng hěn huài. — I'm in a bad mood.

**好吃** hǎochī ↔ **难吃** nánchī — delicious ↔ bad-tasting
这家特别好吃。 Zhè jiā tèbié hǎochī. — This restaurant is especially delicious.
太难吃了。 Tài nánchī le. — It tastes awful.

**新** xīn ↔ **旧** jiù — new ↔ old (things)
我买了一个新手机。 Wǒ mǎi le yí ge xīn shǒujī. — I bought a new phone.
这条路很旧了。 Zhè tiáo lù hěn jiù le. — This road is old.

**容易** róngyì ↔ **难** nán — easy ↔ difficult
中文语法不太难。 Zhōngwén yǔfǎ bú tài nán. — Chinese grammar isn't too hard.
这个很容易。 Zhège hěn róngyì. — This is easy.

**干净** gānjìng ↔ **脏** zāng — clean ↔ dirty
房间很干净。 Fángjiān hěn gānjìng. — The room is clean.
鞋子脏了。 Xiézi zāng le. — The shoes are dirty.

**安全** ānquán ↔ **危险** wēixiǎn — safe ↔ dangerous
这个地方很安全。 Zhège dìfang hěn ānquán. — This place is very safe.
小心，很危险。 Xiǎoxīn, hěn wēixiǎn. — Be careful, it's dangerous.

**对** duì ↔ **错** cuò — correct ↔ wrong
你说得对。 Nǐ shuō de duì. — You're right.
我说错了。 Wǒ shuō cuò le. — I said it wrong.

**一样** yíyàng ↔ **不一样** bù yíyàng — same ↔ different
味道一样的。 Wèidao yíyàng de. — The taste is the same.
这两个不一样。 Zhè liǎng ge bù yíyàng. — These two are different.


## Temperature, Weather & Senses

**热** rè ↔ **冷** lěng — hot ↔ cold
今天好热。 Jīntiān hǎo rè. — It's so hot today.
北方冬天很冷。 Běifāng dōngtiān hěn lěng. — The north is really cold in winter.

**湿** shī ↔ **干** gān — wet/humid ↔ dry
南方很湿。 Nánfāng hěn shī. — The south is very humid.
嘴唇很干。 Zuǐchún hěn gān. — My lips are dry.

**亮** liàng ↔ **暗** àn — bright ↔ dark
房间很亮。 Fángjiān hěn liàng. — The room is bright.
楼道太暗了。 Lóudào tài àn le. — The hallway is too dark.

**吵** chǎo ↔ **安静** ānjìng — loud/noisy ↔ quiet
隔壁太吵了。 Gébì tài chǎo le. — Next door is too noisy.
我想找一个安静的地方。 Wǒ xiǎng zhǎo yí ge ānjìng de dìfang. — I want to find a quiet place.

**辣** là ↔ **不辣** bú là — spicy ↔ not spicy
这个辣不辣？ Zhège là bú là? — Is this spicy?
微辣就好。 Wēi là jiù hǎo. — Mildly spicy is fine.

**甜** tián ↔ **苦** kǔ — sweet ↔ bitter
这个奶茶太甜了。 Zhège nǎichá tài tián le. — This milk tea is too sweet.
这个药很苦。 Zhège yào hěn kǔ. — This medicine is bitter.

**咸** xián ↔ **淡** dàn — salty ↔ bland
有点咸。 Yǒudiǎn xián. — It's a bit salty.
味道太淡了。 Wèidao tài dàn le. — The taste is too bland.

**酸** suān — sour
这个酸奶好酸。 Zhège suānnǎi hǎo suān. — This yogurt is really sour.


## Time & Speed

**快** kuài ↔ **慢** màn — fast ↔ slow
网速很快。 Wǎngsù hěn kuài. — The internet speed is fast.
慢慢来。 Mànmàn lái. — Take your time. / No rush.

**早** zǎo ↔ **晚** wǎn — early ↔ late
你来得很早。 Nǐ lái de hěn zǎo. — You came really early.
不好意思，我来晚了。 Bù hǎoyìsi, wǒ lái wǎn le. — Sorry, I'm late.


## Open / Closed & Distance

**开** kāi ↔ **关** guān — open ↔ closed
店还开着吗？ Diàn hái kāi zhe ma? — Is the shop still open?
已经关门了。 Yǐjīng guānmén le. — It's already closed.

**近** jìn ↔ **远** yuǎn — near ↔ far
地铁站很近。 Dìtiě zhàn hěn jìn. — The metro station is close.
远不远？ Yuǎn bù yuǎn? — Is it far?


## Cost & Age

**贵** guì ↔ **便宜** piányi — expensive ↔ cheap
太贵了吧。 Tài guì le ba. — That's way too expensive.
这个比较便宜。 Zhège bǐjiào piányi. — This one is cheaper.

**年轻** niánqīng ↔ **老** lǎo — young ↔ old (age)
她看起来很年轻。 Tā kàn qǐlái hěn niánqīng. — She looks very young.
这棵树很老了。 Zhè kē shù hěn lǎo le. — This tree is very old.


## Feelings & People

**开心** kāixīn ↔ **难过** nánguò — happy ↔ sad
今天很开心。 Jīntiān hěn kāixīn. — I'm happy today.
他看起来很难过。 Tā kàn qǐlái hěn nánguò. — He looks sad.

**忙** máng ↔ **闲** xián — busy ↔ free/idle
最近特别忙。 Zuìjìn tèbié máng. — I've been really busy recently.
周末有空吗？ Zhōumò yǒu kòng ma? — Are you free this weekend?

**累** lèi ↔ **精神** jīngshen — tired ↔ energetic
好累啊。 Hǎo lèi a. — I'm so tired.
你今天精神很好。 Nǐ jīntiān jīngshen hěn hǎo. — You're full of energy today.

**饿** è ↔ **饱** bǎo — hungry ↔ full
好饿啊。 Hǎo è a. — I'm so hungry.
我吃饱了。 Wǒ chī bǎo le. — I'm full.

**渴** kě — thirsty
好渴，想喝水。 Hǎo kě, xiǎng hē shuǐ. — So thirsty, I want water.

**热闹** rènao ↔ **冷清** lěngqīng — lively/bustling ↔ deserted/quiet
这条街很热闹。 Zhè tiáo jiē hěn rènao. — This street is really lively.
这家店有点冷清。 Zhè jiā diàn yǒudiǎn lěngqīng. — This restaurant is kind of dead.

# 3. CONNECTION WORDS (30 words)

## Joining & Contrasting

**和** hé — and (connecting nouns)
我和朋友一起去。 Wǒ hé péngyou yìqǐ qù. — I'm going with my friend.

**还有** hái yǒu — also / and also / furthermore
还有一件事。 Hái yǒu yí jiàn shì. — There's one more thing.

**或者** huòzhě — or (statements)
你或者他去都行。 Nǐ huòzhě tā qù dōu xíng. — Either you or he can go, both are fine.

**还是** háishi — or (questions / "which one")
你喝咖啡还是茶？ Nǐ hē kāfēi háishi chá? — Do you want coffee or tea?

**但是** dànshì / **可是** kěshì — but
我想去，但是没时间。 Wǒ xiǎng qù, dànshì méi shíjiān. — I want to go, but I don't have time.

**不过** búguò — but / however (softer)
不过也没关系。 Búguò yě méi guānxi. — But it's also fine.

**只** zhǐ / **只是** zhǐshì — only / just
我只是问一下。 Wǒ zhǐshì wèn yíxià. — I'm just asking.

**也** yě — also / too
我也想去。 Wǒ yě xiǎng qù. — I also want to go.

**都** dōu — all / both / even
我们都去。 Wǒmen dōu qù. — We're all going.


## Sequence & Time

**然后** ránhòu — then / after that
先吃饭，然后去。 Xiān chīfàn, ránhòu qù. — Eat first, then go.

**先** xiān — first (do this first)
我先走了。 Wǒ xiān zǒu le. — I'll head out first.

**以前** yǐqián — before (in the past)
我以前住在上海。 Wǒ yǐqián zhù zài Shànghǎi. — I used to live in Shanghai.

**以后** yǐhòu — after / in the future
吃完以后再说。 Chī wán yǐhòu zài shuō. — Let's talk about it after we eat.

**…的时候** …de shíhou — when / at the time of
下雨的时候别出去。 Xiàyǔ de shíhou bié chūqù. — Don't go out when it rains.

**一边…一边** yìbiān…yìbiān — while doing X, also doing Y
我一边吃饭一边看手机。 Wǒ yìbiān chīfàn yìbiān kàn shǒujī. — I eat while looking at my phone.


## Cause & Condition

**因为…所以** yīnwèi…suǒyǐ — because…therefore
因为堵车，所以迟到了。 Yīnwèi dǔchē, suǒyǐ chídào le. — Because of traffic, I was late.

**所以** suǒyǐ — so / therefore
下雨了，所以我没去。 Xiàyǔ le, suǒyǐ wǒ méi qù. — It rained, so I didn't go.

**如果** rúguǒ / **要是** yàoshi — if
如果明天有空，我们去吧。 Rúguǒ míngtiān yǒu kòng, wǒmen qù ba. — If we're free tomorrow, let's go.

**为了** wèile — in order to / for the purpose of
为了学中文，我搬到中国。 Wèile xué zhōngwén, wǒ bān dào Zhōngguó. — I moved to China to learn Chinese.

**虽然…但是** suīrán…dànshì — although…but
虽然很累，但是很开心。 Suīrán hěn lèi, dànshì hěn kāixīn. — Although I'm tired, I'm happy.


## Comparison & Degree

**比** bǐ — compared to / than
今天比昨天热。 Jīntiān bǐ zuótiān rè. — Today is hotter than yesterday.

**最** zuì — most / -est
这个最好吃。 Zhège zuì hǎochī. — This is the most delicious.

**更** gèng — even more
这家更便宜。 Zhè jiā gèng piányi. — This place is even cheaper.

**跟…一样** gēn…yíyàng — same as
跟上次一样。 Gēn shàng cì yíyàng. — Same as last time.

**很** hěn — very (also a default filler before adjectives)
很好。 Hěn hǎo. — Very good.

**太…了** tài…le — too (excessive)
太贵了。 Tài guì le. — Too expensive.

**挺** tǐng — pretty / quite
挺好的。 Tǐng hǎo de. — Pretty good.

**有点** yǒudiǎn — a little bit (slightly negative)
有点远。 Yǒudiǎn yuǎn. — It's a bit far.

**够** gòu — enough
够了够了。 Gòu le gòu le. — That's enough.

**反而** fǎn'ér — instead / on the contrary
没想到反而更贵了。 Méi xiǎngdào fǎn'ér gèng guì le. — Unexpectedly, it's actually more expensive.

# 4. QUESTION WORDS (10 words)

*In Chinese, question words stay in the same position as the answer would go — no need to rearrange the sentence.*

**什么** shénme — what
这是什么？ Zhè shì shénme? — What is this?

**谁** shéi — who
谁告诉你的？ Shéi gàosu nǐ de? — Who told you?

**哪里** nǎlǐ / **哪儿** nǎr — where
厕所在哪里？ Cèsuǒ zài nǎlǐ? — Where is the bathroom?

**哪个** nǎge — which one
你要哪个？ Nǐ yào nǎge? — Which one do you want?

**什么时候** shénme shíhou — when
你什么时候到？ Nǐ shénme shíhou dào? — When do you arrive?

**为什么** wèishénme — why
为什么不行？ Wèishénme bù xíng? — Why not? / Why won't it work?

**怎么** zěnme — how (in what way)
去地铁站怎么走？ Qù dìtiě zhàn zěnme zǒu? — How do I get to the metro?

**怎么样** zěnmeyàng — how is it / what do you think
这家店怎么样？ Zhè jiā diàn zěnmeyàng? — How is this restaurant?

**多少** duōshao — how many / how much
多少钱？ Duōshao qián? — How much (money)?

**几** jǐ — how many (small numbers) / what (time/number)
几点了？ Jǐ diǎn le? — What time is it?

到北京要多久？ Dào Běijīng yào duō jiǔ? — How long does it take to get to Beijing?

**从** cóng — from
我从美国来的。 Wǒ cóng Měiguó lái de. — I come from America.

钥匙在桌子上面。 Yàoshi zài zhuōzi shàngmiàn. — The keys are on the table.

猫在椅子下面。 Māo zài yǐzi xiàmiàn. — The cat is under the chair.

我在咖啡店前面等你。 Wǒ zài kāfēi diàn qiánmiàn děng nǐ. — I'll wait for you in front of the coffee shop.

停车场在后面。 Tíngchēchǎng zài hòumiàn. — The parking lot is in the back.

**旁边** pángbiān — beside / next to

**里面** lǐmiàn — inside
钱包在包里面。 Qiánbāo zài bāo lǐmiàn. — The wallet is inside the bag.

**外面** wàimiàn — outside
我们坐外面吧。 Wǒmen zuò wàimiàn ba. — Let's sit outside.

**中间** zhōngjiān — between / in the middle
他坐在我们中间。 Tā zuò zài wǒmen zhōngjiān. — He's sitting between us.

**附近** fùjìn — nearby
附近有药店吗？ Fùjìn yǒu yàodiàn ma? — Is there a pharmacy nearby?

**对面** duìmiàn — across from / opposite
银行在对面。 Yínháng zài duìmiàn. — The bank is across the street.

**经过** jīngguò — pass by / go through
经过那个路口就到了。 Jīngguò nàge lùkǒu jiù dào le. — You'll be there after passing that intersection.

**关于** guānyú — about / regarding
关于明天的计划… Guānyú míngtiān de jìhuà… — About tomorrow's plan…

**跟** gēn — with (together with someone)
我跟朋友一起来的。 Wǒ gēn péngyou yìqǐ lái de. — I came with my friend.

**离** lí — from (distance)
离这里不远。 Lí zhèlǐ bù yuǎn. — It's not far from here.

**右** yòu — right
往右拐。 Wǎng yòu guǎi. — Turn right.

**左** zuǒ — left
在左边。 Zài zuǒbiān. — It's on the left.

**正在** zhèngzài — right now / in the middle of (progressive marker)
我正在吃饭。 Wǒ zhèngzài chīfàn. — I'm eating right now.

**以后** yǐhòu — later / in the future

**经常** jīngcháng — often
我经常来这里。 Wǒ jīngcháng lái zhèlǐ. — I come here often.

**有时候** yǒushíhou — sometimes

我很少吃甜的。 Wǒ hěn shǎo chī tián de. — I rarely eat sweet things.

**从来没** cónglái méi — never (have never)

**每天** měitiān — every day

# 7. DISCOURSE PARTICLES & SENTENCE-ENDERS (12 words)

*These are the tiny words at the end of Chinese sentences that carry tone and emotion. They're the secret to sounding natural.*

**吗** ma — yes/no question marker
你是中国人吗？ Nǐ shì Zhōngguó rén ma? — Are you Chinese?

**呢** ne — "and you?" / "what about…?" / ongoing state
你呢？ Nǐ ne? — And you?
他在忙呢。 Tā zài máng ne. — He's busy (right now, you know).

**吧** ba — suggestion / assumption / softener
走吧。 Zǒu ba. — Let's go.
应该是吧。 Yīnggāi shì ba. — It should be, I think.

**了** le — completed action / change of state
我吃了。 Wǒ chī le. — I ate.
下雨了。 Xiàyǔ le. — It started raining. (change of state)

**啊** a — exclamation / softener / emphasis
好的啊。 Hǎo de a. — Okay! (warm/enthusiastic)
真的啊？ Zhēn de a? — Really?!

**哦** ó / ò — "oh" / acknowledgment
哦，原来是这样。 Ó, yuánlái shì zhèyàng. — Oh, so that's how it is.

**嘛** ma (neutral tone) — "obviously" / "it goes without saying"
很正常嘛。 Hěn zhèngcháng ma. — It's normal, obviously.

**哈** hā — friendly/playful marker
好的哈。 Hǎo de hā. — Okay (friendly, light).

**嗯** ǹg / ń — mm / uh-huh (acknowledgment)
嗯，我知道了。 Ǹg, wǒ zhīdào le. — Mm, got it.

**哎** āi / ài — hey / sigh
哎，没办法。 Ài, méi bànfǎ. — Sigh, there's nothing we can do.

**喂** wéi — hello (phone only)
喂？你好。 Wéi? Nǐ hǎo. — Hello? (answering a phone call)

**啦** la — combined 了+啊 / cheerful completion
好啦好啦。 Hǎo la hǎo la. — Okay okay, fine fine.

# 8. COMMON NOUNS — DAILY LIFE IN CHINA (55 words)

## Places & Surroundings

**路** lù — road / street
这条路堵得厉害。 Zhè tiáo lù dǔ de lìhai. — This road is badly jammed.

**街** jiē — street (esp. commercial)
这条街很热闹。 Zhè tiáo jiē hěn rènao. — This street is very lively.

**胡同** hútòng — alley / lane (traditional neighborhoods)
这个胡同很有意思。 Zhège hútòng hěn yǒu yìsi. — This hutong is really interesting.

**公园** gōngyuán — park
早上公园里很多人跳舞。 Zǎoshang gōngyuán lǐ hěn duō rén tiàowǔ. — In the morning lots of people dance in the park.

**超市** chāoshì — supermarket
我去超市买点东西。 Wǒ qù chāoshì mǎi diǎn dōngxi. — I'm going to the supermarket to buy some things.

**商场** shāngchǎng — shopping mall
商场里面有吃的吗？ Shāngchǎng lǐmiàn yǒu chī de ma? — Is there food in the mall?

**地铁站** dìtiě zhàn — metro station
最近的地铁站在哪？ Zuìjìn de dìtiě zhàn zài nǎ? — Where's the nearest metro station?

**路口** lùkǒu — intersection
在第二个路口左拐。 Zài dì èr ge lùkǒu zuǒ guǎi. — Turn left at the second intersection.

**小区** xiǎoqū — residential compound
我住在那个小区里。 Wǒ zhù zài nàge xiǎoqū lǐ. — I live in that residential compound.


## Home & Living

**房子** fángzi — house / apartment / place
房子还不错。 Fángzi hái búcuò. — The apartment is pretty nice.

**房间** fángjiān — room
房间有点小。 Fángjiān yǒudiǎn xiǎo. — The room is a bit small.

**厨房** chúfáng — kitchen
厨房可以用吗？ Chúfáng kěyǐ yòng ma? — Can I use the kitchen?

**卫生间** wèishēngjiān — bathroom / restroom
卫生间在哪？ Wèishēngjiān zài nǎ? — Where's the bathroom?

**电** diàn — electricity
停电了。 Tíng diàn le. — Power outage.

**水** shuǐ — water
热水还是凉水？ Rè shuǐ háishi liáng shuǐ? — Hot water or cold water?

**垃圾** lājī — trash / garbage
垃圾桶在哪里？ Lājī tǒng zài nǎlǐ? — Where's the trash can?

**钥匙** yàoshi — key
钥匙忘在家了。 Yàoshi wàng zài jiā le. — I left my keys at home.

**快递** kuàidì — express delivery / package delivery
我的快递到了。 Wǒ de kuàidì dào le. — My delivery arrived.

**外卖** wàimài — food delivery / takeout
我们点外卖吧。 Wǒmen diǎn wàimài ba. — Let's order delivery.


## Transport

**地铁** dìtiě — subway / metro
坐地铁最快。 Zuò dìtiě zuì kuài. — Taking the metro is fastest.

**公交车** gōngjiāochē — bus
坐几路公交车？ Zuò jǐ lù gōngjiāochē? — Which bus number?

**出租车** chūzūchē — taxi
打个出租车吧。 Dǎ ge chūzūchē ba. — Let's take a taxi.

**高铁** gāotiě — high-speed rail
坐高铁去上海很方便。 Zuò gāotiě qù Shànghǎi hěn fāngbiàn. — Taking the high-speed rail to Shanghai is very convenient.

**堵车** dǔchē — traffic jam
又堵车了。 Yòu dǔchē le. — Traffic jam again.


## Weather

**雨** yǔ — rain
看起来要下雨了。 Kàn qǐlái yào xiàyǔ le. — Looks like it's going to rain.

**太阳** tàiyáng — sun
太阳太大了。 Tàiyáng tài dà le. — The sun is too strong.

**风** fēng — wind
今天风很大。 Jīntiān fēng hěn dà. — It's very windy today.

**雾霾** wùmái — smog
今天雾霾挺严重的。 Jīntiān wùmái tǐng yánzhòng de. — The smog is pretty bad today.


## People

**人** rén — person / people
这里人好多。 Zhèlǐ rén hǎo duō. — There are so many people here.

**朋友** péngyou — friend
我和朋友约好了。 Wǒ hé péngyou yuē hǎo le. — I made plans with a friend.

老板，结账。 Lǎobǎn, jiézhàng. — Boss, check please.

**服务员** fúwùyuán — waiter / server
服务员！ Fúwùyuán! — Waiter! (to get attention)

**外国人** wàiguó rén — foreigner
这里外国人不多。 Zhèlǐ wàiguó rén bù duō. — There aren't many foreigners here.

师傅，去机场。 Shīfu, qù jīchǎng. — Driver, to the airport.

**钱** qián — money
带钱了吗？ Dài qián le ma? — Did you bring money?

洗衣服了吗？ Xǐ yīfu le ma? — Did you do the laundry?


## Food & Drink Basics

# 9. TRANSACTION & SITUATION PHRASES (30 phrases)

## Ordering Food & Drinks

**来一份…** lái yí fèn… — I'll have a serving of…
来一份宫保鸡丁。 Lái yí fèn gōngbǎo jīdīng. — I'll have a kung pao chicken.

**我要一个…** wǒ yào yí ge… — I want one…
我要一个冰美式。 Wǒ yào yí ge bīng Měishì. — I want an iced Americano.

**不要…** bú yào… — I don't want… / without…
不要香菜。 Bú yào xiāngcài. — No cilantro.

**少放…** shǎo fàng… — less… / go easy on…
少放辣椒。 Shǎo fàng làjiāo. — Less chili.

**多放…** duō fàng… — extra… / more…
多放醋。 Duō fàng cù. — Extra vinegar.

**微辣/中辣/特辣** wēi là / zhōng là / tè là — mild / medium / extra spicy
中辣吧。 Zhōng là ba. — Medium spicy.

**打包** dǎbāo — to go / doggy bag
剩下的打包。 Shèngxià de dǎbāo. — Pack up the leftovers.

**够了** gòu le — that's enough
够了够了，谢谢。 Gòu le gòu le, xièxie. — That's enough, thanks.


## Paying & Prices

**多少钱？** duōshao qián? — How much?
这个多少钱？ Zhège duōshao qián? — How much is this?

**可以刷卡吗？** kěyǐ shuā kǎ ma? — Can I use a card?
可以刷卡吗？还是只能扫码？ Kěyǐ shuā kǎ ma? Háishi zhǐ néng sǎo mǎ? — Can I use a card? Or only scan to pay?

**扫码支付** sǎo mǎ zhīfù — scan to pay
微信还是支付宝？ Wēixìn háishi Zhīfùbǎo? — WeChat or Alipay?

**结账** jiézhàng — check / bill please
老板，结账。 Lǎobǎn, jiézhàng. — Boss, check please.

**能便宜点吗？** néng piányi diǎn ma? — Can you make it cheaper?
买两个能便宜点吗？ Mǎi liǎng ge néng piányi diǎn ma? — Can you make it cheaper if I buy two?

**开发票** kāi fāpiào — issue a receipt/invoice
能开发票吗？ Néng kāi fāpiào ma? — Can I get an invoice?


## Getting Around

**去…怎么走？** qù…zěnme zǒu? — How do I get to…?
去天安门怎么走？ Qù Tiān'ānmén zěnme zǒu? — How do I get to Tiananmen?

**坐几号线？** zuò jǐ hào xiàn? — Which metro line?
坐几号线到那里？ Zuò jǐ hào xiàn dào nàlǐ? — Which metro line goes there?

**到了叫我一下。** Dào le jiào wǒ yíxià. — Let me know when we arrive.
师傅，到了叫我一下。 Shīfu, dào le jiào wǒ yíxià. — Driver, please tell me when we're there.

**在这儿停就行。** Zài zhèr tíng jiù xíng. — You can stop here.
师傅，在这儿停就行了。 Shīfu, zài zhèr tíng jiù xíng le. — Driver, you can stop here.

**还有多远？** hái yǒu duō yuǎn? — How much further?
到了吗？还有多远？ Dào le ma? Hái yǒu duō yuǎn? — Are we there? How much further?

**导航到…** dǎoháng dào… — navigate to…
你导航到那个餐厅。 Nǐ dǎoháng dào nàge cāntīng. — Navigate to that restaurant.


## Housing & Household

**空调不制冷。** Kōngtiáo bù zhìlěng. — The AC isn't cooling.
空调开了但不制冷。 Kōngtiáo kāi le dàn bù zhìlěng. — The AC is on but not cooling.

**WiFi密码是多少？** WiFi mìmǎ shì duōshao? — What's the WiFi password?
你好，WiFi密码是多少？ Nǐ hǎo, WiFi mìmǎ shì duōshao? — Hi, what's the WiFi password?

**热水器坏了。** Rèshuǐqì huài le. — The water heater is broken.
热水器坏了，没有热水。 Rèshuǐqì huài le, méiyǒu rè shuǐ. — The water heater is broken, there's no hot water.

# 10. NEGATION PATTERNS (8 words)

*Chinese has two main negation words: 不 for habits/choices/future and 没 for past actions/completed events.*

**不** bù — not (general negation — habits, choices, future)
我不喜欢。 Wǒ bù xǐhuan. — I don't like it.
我不去。 Wǒ bú qù. — I'm not going.

**没** méi / **没有** méiyǒu — didn't / haven't (negates past actions)
我没吃早饭。 Wǒ méi chī zǎofàn. — I didn't eat breakfast.
他还没来。 Tā hái méi lái. — He hasn't come yet.

**别** bié — don't (command / telling someone not to)
别着急。 Bié zhāojí. — Don't rush.
别碰！ Bié pèng! — Don't touch!

**不要** bú yào — don't want / don't (prohibition, slightly softer than 别)
不要放味精。 Bú yào fàng wèijīng. — Don't add MSG.

**不用** bú yòng — no need to / don't have to
不用谢。 Bú yòng xiè. — No need to thank me. / You're welcome.
不用等我。 Bú yòng děng wǒ. — No need to wait for me.

**没关系** méi guānxi — it doesn't matter / no problem
没关系没关系。 Méi guānxi méi guānxi. — No problem, no worries.

**从来没** cónglái méi — never (have never done)
我从来没吃过臭豆腐。 Wǒ cónglái méi chī guo chòu dòufu. — I've never eaten stinky tofu.

**不了** bùliǎo — can't (decline politely / indicates inability)
去不了了。 Qù bùliǎo le. — I can't go anymore.
吃不了那么多。 Chī bùliǎo nàme duō. — I can't eat that much.

# 11. COMMON STRUCTURAL PATTERNS (8 patterns)

*These aren't single words — they're grammar frames that are everywhere in spoken Chinese. Recognizing them will massively help your comprehension.*

**V + 过** V + guo — have experienced / have done (at some point in life)
你去过北京吗？ Nǐ qù guo Běijīng ma? — Have you ever been to Beijing?
我吃过，很好吃。 Wǒ chī guo, hěn hǎochī. — I've had it before, it's delicious.

**V + 了** V + le — completed action
我买了两个。 Wǒ mǎi le liǎng ge. — I bought two.

**在 + V** zài + V — currently doing (progressive)
我在看手机。 Wǒ zài kàn shǒujī. — I'm looking at my phone.

**V + 得 + description** V + de + description — how well something is done
他中文说得很好。 Tā zhōngwén shuō de hěn hǎo. — He speaks Chinese really well.
你跑得太快了。 Nǐ pǎo de tài kuài le. — You ran too fast.

**V + 不 + result** / **V + 得 + result** — potential complement (can/can't achieve result)
听不懂。 Tīng bù dǒng. — I can't understand (by listening).
看得见吗？ Kàn de jiàn ma? — Can you see it?

**把 + object + V** bǎ + object + V — do something to a specific thing
把门关上。 Bǎ mén guān shàng. — Close the door.
把这个给他。 Bǎ zhège gěi tā. — Give this to him.

**是…的** shì…de — emphasizing when/where/how something happened
我是坐飞机来的。 Wǒ shì zuò fēijī lái de. — I came by plane (emphasis on how).
你是什么时候到的？ Nǐ shì shénme shíhou dào de? — When did you arrive? (emphasis on when)

**越来越** yuè lái yuè — more and more
天气越来越热了。 Tiānqì yuè lái yuè rè le. — The weather is getting hotter and hotter.
你中文越来越好了。 Nǐ zhōngwén yuè lái yuè hǎo le. — Your Chinese is getting better and better.

哎呀，我忘了！ Āiyā, wǒ wàng le! — Oh no, I forgot!

天哪，这么贵！ Tiān na, zhème guì! — Oh my god, this expensive!

哦，这样啊。 Ó, zhèyàng a. — Oh, I see.

那就算了吧。 Nà jiù suàn le ba. — Well then, forget it.

算了算了，不说了。 Suàn le suàn le, bù shuō le. — Forget it, let's not talk about it.

**对对对** duì duì duì — right right right / exactly

**差不多** chàbuduō — close enough / pretty much

**走吧** zǒu ba — let's go
好了，走吧。 Hǎo le, zǒu ba. — Alright, let's go.

**加油** jiāyóu — you've got this / keep going / fighting!
加油！你可以的。 Jiāyóu! Nǐ kěyǐ de. — You've got this! You can do it.
